About Us

Deep Shikha Group has a fleet of 24 trucks mounted with containers which operates for transportation of high-value cargo and has a large fleet of 400 attached trucks. Has proven experience in carrying FMCG, Food Products, Machinery, Engineering Goods, Glass, Steel, and Automotive industry. Delivering goods with 100% accuracy and safety and a record of over 90%+ on time deliveries Real time reporting on delivery status Adherence to safety, health, environment (SHE) guidelines as provided by the customers and government of India Drivers are well equipped and trained to operate digital tools to coordinate with central team in an event of any mishap or urgency.

Our Services

The Company’s specialization is transportation of Full Truck Loads, ODC consignments and operations through Multi-modal services.
